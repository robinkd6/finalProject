How It Works

With the IBM Watson's API, we'll be able to determine your personaility on the basis of The Big 5: Openess, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness and Neuroticism.

Simply type out a few paragraphs describing who you are (i.e. Who you are, where you were born, likes/dislikes etc.) and we'll analyze certain keywords then display your Big 5 percentages!
